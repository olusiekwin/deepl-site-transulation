# Images to source for Quantavus

Place files in `public/images/` (and `public/images/partners/` for partner logos).

---

## What to search for (stock sites)

Use these on **Unsplash**, **Pexels**, **Shutterstock**, **Getty**, etc.:

| Use | Search terms |
|-----|----------------|
| **Hero** | `corporate office building`, `financial district skyline`, `business headquarters glass`, `city skyline professional`, `executive boardroom`, `modern office exterior` |
| **Financial planning** | `business strategy meeting`, `financial planning documents`, `executive handshake`, `corporate presentation`, `investment discussion`, `laptop charts finance` |
| **Infrastructure** | `infrastructure construction`, `wind turbines energy`, `solar panels industrial`, `port cargo logistics`, `highway construction`, `power plant` |
| **Global finance** | `global business handshake`, `world map business`, `international meeting`, `diverse team office`, `corporate global network` |
| **OG / social** | Create a 1200×630 graphic with your logo + tagline (no stock photo). |

**Style:** Corporate, clean, minimal. Prefer: *professional*, *executive*, *modern office*, *finance*, *business*. Avoid cluttered or casual.

## Format summary
- **Photos:** JPG, good quality, avoid heavy compression.
- **Logos:** PNG with transparency, sRGB.
- **Naming:** Use the exact filenames above so the app finds them.
